Javabhumi Story


‘Farm to cup coffee trade’

Indonesia has long established its position as one of the leading coffee producer in the world, with its well-renowned beans such as Aceh Gayo, Sulawesi Toraja, and the famous Luwak Coffee. Bringing the ideas of opening up the wonder of Indonesian coffee industry to the world, Javanagari dreamed of expanding the coffee horizon from Indonesia to the international market.

From the word ‘Java’, one of the major coffee-producing island in Indonesia (And which word has also become synonimous as coffee internationally), and ‘Nagari’ which means country, as we carried beans from all over the corner of Indonesia. On our mission, we want to introduce a direct line straight from the farmer to the world. From their own soil, we handpicked each and every beans that goes into Javanagari, ensuring only the most top quality beans of its class

Be it a home industry, an F&B corporation, or a personal enthusiast, we have it all for you. With Javanagari, explore the caffeine wonder of Indonesia, grown with love from indigenous farm straight to your cup.

Javanagari

Javanagari was established back in early 2022 with the mission to empower our local community & farmers. We understand that Indonesia is regarded as one of the best coffee bean producer worldwide, therefore we would like to bring it into your cup as well! We partner with trustworthy farmer communities to deliver a good quality cherries. Our experience will ensure the excellence in processing the cherry into high-quality beans

Fine Process

Every procedure is carried on considerately to meet a certain standard. Starting from growing the plants, harvesting, and quality control in our processing facilities. Our products vary widely depend on the planting location, washing & drying methods, and roasting process. Most importantly, we only pick up the ripe red cherries to maintain the sustainablity of our coffee ecosystem.
